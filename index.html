Learn to Solve a Rubik's Cube

Step-by-step guide for complete beginners. Learn the basics of solving a 3×3 cube.

Step 1: Solve White Corners

Find a white corner piece. Position it above the spot it needs to go, then apply the algorithm.

White on Right
R U R' U'
White on Left
L' U' L U
Step 2: Solve White Edges

Match an edge to its center. Face it toward you and apply the algorithm.

White needs to go Right
U (Righty Alg) → R U R' U'
White needs to go Left
U' (Lefty Alg) → L' U' L U
Step 3: Yellow Cross

Look at the yellow edges and apply the correct algorithm:

Yellow Line
F R U R' U' F'
Yellow Hook
F R U R' U' F'
Yellow Dot
F R U R' U' F' (twice)
Step 4: Solve Yellow Edges

Align two yellow edges to their correct center:

Adjacent Edges
R U R' U R U2 R'
Diagonal Edges
R U R' U R U2 R' (twice)
Step 5: Match Yellow Corners

Bring yellow corners to their correct spot (matching the colors on the sides):

Niklas
R U' L' U R' U' L
Step 6: Solve Yellow Corners

1. Hold solved corner in bottom right 2. Apply Righty Alg until solved 3. Turn bottom layer 4. Repeat

Corner Twist
R U R' U'
Cube Notation

Learn what each letter means in cube algorithms.

Basic Face Moves (Clockwise)
R, L, U
Right, Left, Up clockwise
D, F, B
Down, Front, Back clockwise
Prime Moves (Counterclockwise) & Double Moves
R', R2, r
Prime = counterclockwise, 2 = 180°, lowercase = double layer
Cube Rotations
x, y, z
Rotate entire cube on R, U, F axes
Slice Turns
M, S, E
M=between L/R, S=between F/B, E=between U/D
Notation Reference
R - Right
Turn Right face clockwise
L - Left
Turn Left face clockwise
U - Up
Turn Up (top) face clockwise
D - Down
Turn Down (bottom) face clockwise
F - Front
Turn Front face clockwise
B - Back
Turn Back face clockwise
' (prime)
Counterclockwise turn
2 (double)
180° half turn
Lowercase
Double layer turn
